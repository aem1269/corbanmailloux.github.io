MediaList is the love-child of TVList and MovieList. It is a simple C# movie and TV show listing application, specifically for collections spanning multiple drives or folders.
It does this by quickly scanning through user-defined sets of directories, indexing files and folders inside, and creating sorted, searchable lists of movies and TV shows.
It features real-time searching and the ability to open a selection in Windows Explorer.

The code, and a compiled EXE file, are available on GitHub.
Any questions can be directed at MediaList@corb.co.
